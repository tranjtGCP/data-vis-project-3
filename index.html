Gravity Falls

Gravity Falls is an American cartoon that aired on Disney channels from 2012 to 2016. It is a mystery comedy that follows the lives of Dipper, Mabel, and Stan Pines along with the paranormal activities that occur at the titular location.
The show ran for two seasons, with a total of 40 episodes. The main cast, consists of twin siblings Dipper and Mabel, their great uncle Stan, their friends Wendy, Soos, Robbie, and Ford.
